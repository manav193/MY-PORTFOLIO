VELDORA BITES

A full restaurant website implementation evolved from the Velora Bites design direction into a multi-page, portfolio-ready ordering experience.

Problem

Translate a premium restaurant concept into a practical product flow without losing the visual identity: discovery on Home, a dedicated Menu, persistent cart feedback, and a separate Orders experience.

Product structure

  • Home focused on introduction and daily specials.
  • Dedicated Menu page with broad vegetarian categories and INR pricing.
  • Persistent cart/order bar across shopping pages, hidden where it would be redundant on Orders.
  • Coupons plus card and UPI offer presentation.
  • Separate Orders page and clear navigation between browsing and checkout flow.

Safety decision

The live portfolio implementation intentionally keeps payment interactions demonstrative rather than accepting real payments. That avoids exposing a portfolio prototype as a real commerce endpoint.
