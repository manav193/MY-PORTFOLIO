Flora & Flavor

An immersive luxury vegetarian restaurant concept combining botanical art direction with scroll-driven storytelling and practical restaurant interactions.

Problem

Create a premium hospitality experience that feels cinematic without turning navigation, menu discovery, reservations, or ordering into decorative friction.

Design & interaction

The centerpiece is an Apple-style pinned hero sequence using GSAP and ScrollTrigger. A bowl rotates and scales with scroll while typography reveals in a staggered sequence, moving the experience from visual introduction into brand storytelling.

Development

  • Semantic HTML, modern CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
  • Filterable seasonal menu and interactive order controls.
  • Reservation validation and confirmation feedback.
  • Responsive navigation, gallery, reviews, location content, and reduced-motion support.
  • Single-file static architecture for simple deployment.

Result

The project demonstrates cinematic motion tied to a usable information architecture. Reservation and ordering are intentionally front-end demonstrations and do not process real bookings or payments.
