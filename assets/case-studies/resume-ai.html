resume-ai

A compact AI-oriented resume project retained in the portfolio as an experiment rather than inflated into a flagship product.

Purpose

The repository represents exploration around applying AI to resume-related workflows and is included for completeness of the public GitHub body of work.

Scope

Compared with larger projects such as ToolVerse, SELFYY, SHIFT-ZERO, and FATE AI, this repository is much smaller. The case study therefore documents it proportionally instead of claiming production depth that the repository does not demonstrate.

Takeaway

Its portfolio role is to show iteration breadth and experimentation while the larger case studies carry the evidence for architecture, interaction design, deployment, testing, and production engineering.
